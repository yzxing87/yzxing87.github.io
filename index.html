About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at HKUST, supervised by Prof. Qifeng Chen. Before that, I received my bachelor degree from Wuhan University. During my PhD, I have been fortunate to do long research internships at NVIDIA Research (LPR team), and Tencent (Applied Research Center).

I am interested in computer vision and deep learning. In particular, my research focuses on computational photography, image and video processing, image and video generation, and other low-level vision problems.

I am now on the job market, looking for a research scientist / engineer or post-doc position starting from 2024. Please feel free to reach out to me if you have any opennings.
